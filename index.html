The Challenge

Some Stories
Are Too Big
to Walk Past

Many elderly visitors and people with mobility challenges find it difficult to fully experience a sprawling museum. Language barriers further limit their connection with history.

"Every visitor deserves to feel the full weight of Gandhi Ji's journey — not just read about it."
01

Mobility Barriers
